琉璃 - 代表品种 Glass Art - Representative Products

代表品种

博山琉璃根据制作工艺和用途的不同，可以分为多个代表品种。这些品种在造型、色彩和工艺上各有特色，共同构成了丰富多彩的琉璃艺术体系。

按工艺方法分，主要有吹制琉璃、压制琉璃、拉制琉璃、铸造琉璃等；按用途分，主要有装饰琉璃、实用琉璃、艺术琉璃等。

这些代表品种不仅体现了琉璃制作技艺的多样性，也反映了琉璃在不同历史时期和文化背景下的发展变化。

Representative Products

According to different production techniques and uses, Boshan Glass can be divided into multiple representative varieties. These varieties have their own characteristics in shape, color, and craftsmanship, together forming a rich and colorful glass art system.

According to craft methods, they mainly include blown glass, pressed glass, drawn glass, cast glass, etc.; according to uses, they mainly include decorative glass, practical glass, artistic glass, etc.

These representative varieties not only reflect the diversity of glass making techniques, but also reflect the development and changes of glass in different historical periods and cultural backgrounds.
