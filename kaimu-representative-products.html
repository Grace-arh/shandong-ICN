楷木雕刻 - 代表品种 Camphor Wood Carving - Representative Products

代表品种

楷木雕刻的代表品种主要包括以下几类：首先是人物雕像，尤其是孔子及其弟子的雕像，这是楷木雕刻最具代表性的产品。其次是文房四宝，如笔筒、砚台、笔架等，这些产品不仅实用，还具有很高的艺术价值。

此外，还有印章、屏风、挂屏等装饰性物品，以及各种祭祀用品。这些产品形式多样，工艺精湛，充分体现了楷木雕刻的艺术魅力和文化内涵。

Representative Products

The representative products of Camphor Wood Carving mainly include the following categories: First, there are figure statues, especially the statues of Confucius and his disciples, which are the most representative products of Camphor Wood Carving. Second, there are the four treasures of the study, such as pen holders, ink slabs, pen racks, etc., which are not only practical but also have high artistic value.

In addition, there are seals, screens, wall hangings and other decorative items, as well as various sacrificial supplies. These products are diverse in form and exquisite in craftsmanship, fully reflecting the artistic charm and cultural connotation of Camphor Wood Carving.

主要类型

雕像、印章、文房四宝等

Statues, seals, four treasures of the study, etc.

用途范围

祭祀、收藏、装饰等

Sacrifice, collection, decoration, etc.

尺寸规格

大小不一，形态各异

Various sizes and shapes
