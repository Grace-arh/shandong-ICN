花馍 - 代表品种 Flower Steamed Bun - Representative Products

代表品种

山东花馍根据使用场合和造型特点，可以分为多个代表品种。这些品种在不同地区有着不同的特色和寓意，共同构成了丰富多彩的山东花馍文化。

按使用场合分，主要有婚礼花馍、寿宴花馍、节日花馍等；按造型特点分，主要有花卉造型花馍、动物造型花馍、人物造型花馍等。

这些代表品种不仅在民间广泛流传，还成为了山东非物质文化遗产的重要组成部分，展示了山东人民的智慧和创造力。

Representative Products

Shandong Flower Steamed Bun can be divided into multiple representative types according to their usage occasions and shape characteristics. These types have different characteristics and meanings in different regions, together forming the rich and colorful Shandong Flower Steamed Bun culture.

According to usage occasions, they are mainly wedding buns, birthday celebration buns, festival buns, etc.; according to shape characteristics, they are mainly flower-shaped buns, animal-shaped buns, character-shaped buns, etc.

These representative products not only widely spread among the people, but also become an important part of Shandong's intangible cultural heritage, showcasing the wisdom and creativity of the Shandong people.
