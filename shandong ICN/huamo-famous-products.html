花馍 - 著名品种 Flower Steamed Bun - Famous Products

代表品种

菏泽龙凤馍、寿桃馍、枣山馍等

Heze Dragon and Phoenix Buns, Peach Buns, etc.

著名品种

山东花馍有许多著名的品种，如菏泽的龙凤馍、寿桃馍、枣山馍，聊城的面老虎，潍坊的面鱼等。其中，菏泽的龙凤馍最具代表性，造型精美，工艺精湛，是花馍中的精品。

这些花馍品种各具特色，反映了山东不同地区的文化传统和审美情趣，共同构成了丰富多彩的山东花馍文化。

Famous Types

There are many famous types of Shandong Flower Steamed Bun, such as dragon and phoenix buns, longevity peach buns, and jujube mountain buns in Heze, face tigers in Liaocheng, and face fish in Weifang. Among them, the dragon and phoenix buns in Heze are the most representative, with exquisite shapes and craftsmanship, and are the finest products of Flower Steamed Bun.

These types of Flower Steamed Bun have their own characteristics, reflecting the cultural traditions and aesthetic tastes of different regions in Shandong, and together constitute the rich and colorful Shandong Flower Steamed Bun culture.
