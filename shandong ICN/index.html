Shandong ICH Overview

Shandong's intangible cultural heritage is a precious spiritual wealth created by the people of Qilu in long-term production and life practices. It covers ten categories including folk literature, traditional music, traditional dance, traditional drama, folk art, traditional sports, entertainment and acrobatics, traditional fine arts, traditional skills, traditional medicine, and folk customs. Up to now, Shandong Province has 186 national-level intangible cultural heritage representative projects and 1,073 provincial-level intangible cultural heritage representative projects.

Cultural Significance of Shandong ICH

Shandong Intangible Cultural Heritage is an important part of Qilu culture, the crystallization of the wisdom of the people of Shandong, and carries rich historical information and cultural genes. Protecting and inheriting Shandong Intangible Cultural Heritage is of great significance for carrying forward China's excellent traditional culture, enhancing cultural confidence, and promoting cultural innovation and development.

Shandong ICH is not only a witness to history, but also an important resource for contemporary cultural construction. Through the protection and inheritance of Shandong ICH, we can promote the development of cultural diversity, enrich people's spiritual and cultural life, and enhance national cohesion and centripetal force.

"ICH is the spiritual lifeblood of the Chinese nation and an important source for cultivating socialist core values."
