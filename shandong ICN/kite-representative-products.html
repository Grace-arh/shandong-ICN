代表品种

潍坊风筝的代表品种丰富多样，其中最具特色的是龙头蜈蚣风筝。这种风筝以龙头为主体，后面连接着数十节甚至上百节的蜈蚣身，长度可达数百米，放飞时如巨龙腾空，蔚为壮观。

除了龙头蜈蚣风筝外，硬翅风筝也是潍坊风筝的重要代表。硬翅风筝的翅膀坚硬，造型简洁，飞行稳定，常见的有沙燕风筝等。软翅风筝的翅膀柔软，飞行时更加灵活，常见的有鹰风筝、蝴蝶风筝等。

板子风筝则是最基础的风筝类型，形状多为方形或圆形，结构简单，易于制作和放飞，适合初学者。

Representative Products

Weifang Kite has a rich variety of representative products, among which the most distinctive is the dragon-headed centipede kite. This kite takes the dragon head as the main body, followed by dozens or even hundreds of centipede segments, with a length of hundreds of meters. When flying, it looks like a giant dragon soaring in the sky, which is spectacular.

In addition to the dragon-headed centipede kite, the hard wing kite is also an important representative of Weifang Kite. Hard wing kites have hard wings, simple shapes, and stable flight. Common ones include swallow kites. Soft wing kites have soft wings and are more flexible when flying. Common ones include eagle kites and butterfly kites.

Board kites are the most basic type of kites, mostly square or round in shape, with simple structures, easy to make and fly, suitable for beginners.
