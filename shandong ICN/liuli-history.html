琉璃 - 历史渊源 Glass Art - Historical Origin

起源时期

商周时期

Shang and Zhou Dynasties

历史渊源

博山琉璃的历史可以追溯到3000多年前的商周时期，是中国最早的琉璃产地之一。早在春秋战国时期，博山琉璃就已经成为重要的手工艺品，被用作贵族的装饰品和礼器。

明清时期，博山琉璃产业达到鼎盛，出现了许多著名的琉璃作坊和艺人。清代乾隆年间，博山琉璃被列为宫廷贡品，工艺水平达到了前所未有的高度。

Historical Origin

The history of Boshan Glass can be traced back to more than 3,000 years ago during the Shang and Zhou Dynasties, and it is one of the earliest glass producing areas in China. As early as the Spring and Autumn Period and Warring States Period, Boshan Glass had become an important handicraft, used as ornaments and ritual vessels for nobles.

During the Ming and Qing Dynasties, the Boshan Glass industry reached its peak, with many famous glass workshops and artisans emerging. During the Qianlong period of the Qing Dynasty, Boshan Glass was listed as a tribute to the imperial court, and the craft level reached an unprecedented height.
