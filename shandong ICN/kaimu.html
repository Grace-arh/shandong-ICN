楷木雕刻 Camphor Wood Carving

简介

楷木雕刻是中国传统木雕艺术的重要代表，起源于2500多年前的孔子故里曲阜。它以曲阜地区特产的楷木为原料，通过精湛的手工雕刻技艺，创作出各种精美的艺术品，是儒家文化的重要载体。

Introduction

Camphor Wood Carving is an important representative of traditional Chinese wood carving art, originating from Qufu, the hometown of Confucius more than 2,500 years ago. Using camphor wood, a specialty of the Qufu area, as raw material, it creates various exquisite artworks through superb manual carving techniques and is an important carrier of Confucian culture.
