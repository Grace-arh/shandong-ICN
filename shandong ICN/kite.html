风筝 Kite

风筝简介

潍坊风筝是山东省潍坊市的传统手工艺品，有着2000多年的历史，被誉为"世界风筝之都"。潍坊风筝制作工艺精湛，造型优美，色彩艳丽，是中国国家级非物质文化遗产。

潍坊国际风筝节始于1984年，每年4月举办，是世界上规模最大、影响最广的风筝节，吸引了来自世界各地的风筝爱好者参与。

Introduction to Kite

Weifang Kite is a traditional handicraft from Weifang City, Shandong Province, with a history of more than 2,000 years. It is known as the "World Kite Capital". Weifang Kite is renowned for its exquisite craftsmanship, beautiful shape and bright colors, and is a national intangible cultural heritage of China.

The Weifang International Kite Festival, which began in 1984 and is held every April, is the largest and most influential kite festival in the world, attracting kite enthusiasts from all over the world to participate.
