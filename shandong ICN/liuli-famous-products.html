琉璃 - 著名产品 Glass Art - Famous Products

著名产品

博山琉璃有许多著名的产品，如琉璃花球、琉璃内画、琉璃花瓶、琉璃摆件等。其中，琉璃内画是博山琉璃的一大特色，它是在琉璃瓶内部进行绘画，技艺精湛，画面精美，被誉为"中华一绝"。

琉璃花球也是博山琉璃的代表性产品之一，它是通过多层玻璃液叠加、拉伸、旋转等工艺制作而成，内部呈现出各种美丽的图案，如花卉、山水、人物等。

Famous Products

Boshan Glass has many famous products, such as glass flower balls, glass inner paintings, glass vases, glass ornaments, etc. Among them, glass inner painting is a major feature of Boshan Glass. It is painted inside glass bottles with exquisite skills and beautiful pictures, known as "a unique skill of China".

Glass flower balls are also one of the representative products of Boshan Glass. They are made through multi-layer glass liquid stacking, stretching, rotating and other processes, and show various beautiful patterns inside, such as flowers, landscapes, figures, etc.
