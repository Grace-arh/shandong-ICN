鲁绣 - 代表品种 Lu Embroidery - Representative Products

主要品种

衣线绣、发丝绣、金银绣

Clothing line embroidery, hair embroidery, gold and silver embroidery

分布地区

济南、青岛、烟台等地

Jinan, Qingdao, Yantai, etc.

代表品种

鲁绣有许多著名的品种，其中最具代表性的包括衣线绣、发丝绣和金银绣。

衣线绣是鲁绣中最古老的品种之一，以粗硬的丝线为主要材料，绣品厚重结实，具有浓郁的民间特色。

发丝绣则是鲁绣的一大特色，使用人的头发作为绣线，绣品细腻逼真，具有很高的艺术价值。

金银绣是鲁绣中最奢华的品种，使用金线和银线进行装饰，绣品华贵典雅，常用于宫廷服饰和高级礼品。这些代表品种各具特色，共同构成了丰富多彩的鲁绣文化。

Representative Products

Lu Embroidery has many famous varieties, among which the most representative include clothing line embroidery, hair embroidery and gold and silver embroidery.

Clothing line embroidery is one of the oldest varieties in Lu Embroidery, using thick and hard silk thread as the main material. The embroidery is thick and solid, with strong folk characteristics.

Hair embroidery is a major feature of Lu Embroidery, using human hair as embroidery thread. The embroidery is delicate and realistic, with high artistic value.

Gold and silver embroidery is the most luxurious variety in Lu Embroidery, using gold and silver threads for decoration. The embroidery is luxurious and elegant, and is often used in court costumes and high-end gifts. These representative varieties each have their own characteristics, together forming the rich and colorful Lu Embroidery culture.
