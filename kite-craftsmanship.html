风筝 - 制作工艺 Kite - Craftsmanship

制作步骤

扎、糊、绘、放

Binding, Pasting, Painting, Flying

制作工艺

潍坊风筝的制作工艺包括"扎、糊、绘、放"四大步骤。扎制骨架是基础，采用精选的竹子，经过烘烤、弯曲、绑扎等工序制成；糊纸是关键，使用优质的宣纸或绢；绘画是灵魂，运用传统的工笔画技法；放飞是目的，讲究技巧和经验。

风筝的骨架结构有多种形式，如硬翅、软翅、板子、串式等，每种结构都有其独特的制作方法和飞行特点。风筝的绘画题材广泛，包括历史人物、神话传说、花鸟鱼虫、吉祥图案等，其中最具特色的是龙头蜈蚣风筝。

Craftsmanship

The production process of Weifang Kite includes four major steps: "binding, pasting, painting and flying". Binding the skeleton is the foundation, using selected bamboo, which is made through baking, bending, binding and other processes; pasting paper is the key, using high-quality rice paper or silk; painting is the soul, using traditional gongbi painting techniques; flying is the purpose, emphasizing skills and experience.

Kite skeleton structures have various forms, such as hard wing, soft wing, board, string type, etc. Each structure has its unique production method and flight characteristics. The painting themes of kites are wide-ranging, including historical figures, myths and legends, flowers, birds, fish and insects, auspicious patterns, etc. Among them, the most distinctive is the dragon-headed centipede kite.

主要材料

竹子、宣纸、绢、颜料

Bamboo, Rice Paper, Silk, Pigments
