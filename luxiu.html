鲁绣 Lu Embroidery

鲁绣简介

鲁绣是中国历史最悠久的刺绣工艺之一，起源于春秋战国时期，是齐鲁文化的重要组成部分。它具有粗犷豪放、厚重结实的艺术风格，与江南刺绣的细腻柔美形成鲜明对比。

鲁绣采用多种针法，包括平针、打籽、盘金、滚针、锁针、网针等20多种针法，绣线以蚕丝线和金线为主，题材广泛，包括花卉、鸟兽、人物、山水、历史故事等。

Introduction to Lu Embroidery

Lu Embroidery is one of the oldest embroidery techniques in China, originating from the Spring and Autumn Period and Warring States Period. It is an important part of Qilu culture with bold, unconstrained, thick and solid artistic style, which contrasts sharply with the delicate and soft style of Jiangnan embroidery.

Lu Embroidery uses more than 20 kinds of stitches, including flat stitch, seed stitch, gold plate stitch, rolling stitch, lock stitch, net stitch, etc. The embroidery threads are mainly silk threads and gold threads, with wide-ranging themes including flowers, birds and animals, characters, landscapes, historical stories, etc.
